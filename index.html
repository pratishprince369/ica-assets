Answers to Your Property Management Concerns

Yes, absolutely. ICA Asset Management acts as your local legal representative on the ground. We manage tenant searches, property repairs, rent collection, tax filing coordinates, and regular security checkups. You receive all updates, contracts, and video inspection logs via our digital dashboard and WhatsApp, ensuring full control from abroad.

We execute scheduled physical inspections. During these audits, our representatives create high-resolution photographic and video documentation of all rooms, structures, boundaries, and utility connections. These media files and an inspection checklist report are compiled and sent directly to you via WhatsApp or email.

We actively manage properties across major cities in Punjab, India (headquartered in Ludhiana, covering Jalandhar, Amritsar, Patiala, and surrounding areas). For our Canadian NRI clients, we have a corporate service office in Brampton, Ontario, where you can meet us in person to execute legal paperwork and discuss your portfolio.

We perform exhaustive background checks, verify identities via Government IDs, and draft robust rental agreements registered locally. Rent is collected digitally on a set date each month and transferred directly to your designated bank account. If maintenance issues or disputes arise, our local team coordinates repairs and resolves conflicts swiftly.
